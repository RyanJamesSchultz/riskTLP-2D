
This folder contains processed data related to the formation outline and true vertical depth of formation, digitized from a number of sources.  For additional information, please read prior works [Schultz et al., 2021].

Reference:
R. Schultz, G. Beroza, W. Ellsworth. (2021). A strategy for choosing red-light thresholds to manage hydraulic fracturing induced seismicity in North America. Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth, XX, doi: xx.
